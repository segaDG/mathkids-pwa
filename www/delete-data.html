🗑️ Delete Your MathKids Account & Data

Last updated: April 10, 2026

This page explains how to permanently delete your MathKids account and all associated data. We respect your right to privacy and provide multiple ways to delete your data at any time.

📱 Method 1: Delete From Within the App (Recommended)

Steps to delete your account in the app:
  1. Open the MathKids app on your device
  2. From the home screen, tap the "Avatar" button (pink circle with character)
  3. Scroll to the bottom of the character selection modal
  4. Tap the red "🗑 Delete Account" button
  5. A confirmation prompt will appear — type the word "DELETE" (or "HAPUS" for Indonesian, "PADAM" for Malay, "SUPPRIMER" for French, "削除" for Japanese)
  6. Tap OK to confirm

Once confirmed, your account and all data will be permanently deleted within seconds. You will be returned to the onboarding screen.

⚠️ Warning: This action is permanent and cannot be undone. All progress, stars, coins, and unlocked characters will be lost.

📧 Method 2: Email Request

If you cannot access the app or prefer to request deletion via email:

Send an email to:

📧 sega1902@gmail.com

Include in your email:

  • Subject: Delete MathKids Account
  • Your User Code (format: MK-XXXXXX) — found in the home screen below your name
  • OR your parent email if you provided one for account recovery

We will process your request within 7 business days and send you a confirmation email once deleted.

🔒 What Data is Retained (and Why)

None. MathKids does not retain any user data after account deletion. We do not keep:

  • ❌ Backup copies
  • ❌ Anonymized records
  • ❌ Analytics data linked to your account
  • ❌ Email logs

The only exception is server access logs (timestamps, IP addresses) which are automatically purged by our hosting provider (Supabase) within 30 days as part of standard infrastructure operations. These logs are not linked to specific users.

👶 For Parents

If you are a parent or legal guardian and want to delete your child's MathKids account:

  1. Use Method 1 above (delete from within the app) — accessible from the parent zone
  2. OR use Method 2 (email request) — include your relationship to the child in the email

We will process parental deletion requests with the highest priority, in compliance with COPPA (Children's Online Privacy Protection Act) and GDPR Article 17 (Right to Erasure).

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

Can I recover my account after deletion?

No. Account deletion is permanent. If you change your mind later, you'll need to start a new account.

Will my Google Play subscription be canceled?

No. Account deletion in MathKids does NOT cancel your Google Play subscription. To cancel your subscription:

  1. Open Google Play Store
  2. Go to your profile → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions
  3. Tap MathKids → Cancel subscription

How long does deletion take?

In-app deletion: Immediate (a few seconds)
Email request: Within 7 business days

What if I just want to delete some data, not all?

Currently, MathKids only supports complete account deletion. If you need partial deletion, please contact us at sega1902@gmail.com.
