🎓 MathKids Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 10, 2026

MathKids ("we", "our", "the app") is a children's educational math learning Progressive Web App. We are committed to protecting the privacy of our users, especially children.

👶 Designed for children ages 3-10. This app complies with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA) and Google Play's Families Policy.

1. Information We Collect

Information stored locally on your device:

  • Display name (e.g., "Leo") — chosen by user during onboarding
  • Age — used to set appropriate difficulty level
  • Country — used for currency display in money lessons
  • Language preference
  • Game progress — stars, levels completed, scores
  • Coins earned in-app (no real money)
  • Daily login streak

Information stored on our servers (Supabase):

  • Anonymous user ID (e.g., "MK-A4B2X9") — for cloud sync and leaderboards
  • Display name and chosen mascot — to display on global leaderboards
  • Game scores — for leaderboard rankings
  • Total stars and coins — for sync across devices
  • Parent email (optional) — only if parent voluntarily provides it for account recovery

Information we do NOT collect:

  • ❌ Real names
  • ❌ Phone numbers
  • ❌ Home address
  • ❌ GPS location
  • ❌ Photos or camera access
  • ❌ Microphone access
  • ❌ Contact lists
  • ❌ Browsing history
  • ❌ Device identifiers (IMEI, MAC address)

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide personalized math lessons appropriate for the child's age
  • Save game progress so children can continue where they left off
  • Display global and country leaderboards for friendly competition
  • Sync data across multiple devices
  • Allow account recovery if device is lost (only if parent email is provided)

3. No Advertising

✅ MathKids does NOT show third-party advertisements.

✅ MathKids does NOT use behavioral tracking or analytics SDKs.

✅ MathKids does NOT share data with advertisers.

4. In-App Purchases

MathKids offers an optional Premium subscription that unlocks additional categories (Multiplication, Division, Fractions, Shapes, Time, Money). The subscription:

  • Is processed through the Google Play Billing system
  • Is protected behind a Parent Gate (PIN entry)
  • Can be canceled anytime from Google Play settings

In-game coins are earned through gameplay only and have no real-world value.

5. Parental Controls

  • Parent Zone — protected by 4-digit PIN (default: 1234, can be changed)
  • Account Deletion — parents can delete their child's account at any time from the profile screen
  • No external links — children cannot navigate outside the app
  • Inappropriate name filter — names are filtered for offensive language in 5 languages

6. Data Storage and Security

  • Local data is stored on the device using browser localStorage
  • Cloud data is stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL database) with encrypted connections (HTTPS)
  • Data is retained until the user deletes their account or uninstalls the app
  • We do not sell or share data with third parties

7. Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

We comply with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

  • We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13 without parental consent
  • The display name chosen by the child is not considered personal information as it is not linked to real identity
  • Parents can review, delete, or refuse further collection of their child's information by deleting the account from within the app

8. Your Rights

As a parent or legal guardian, you have the right to:

  • Access your child's data — visible in the app's Stats and Profile screens
  • Delete all data — use "Delete Account" button in the Profile modal
  • Export data — contact us for data export requests
  • Refuse further collection — uninstall the app

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the new policy.
